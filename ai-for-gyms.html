The Real Problem

You're Running a Leaky
Membership Bucket.

Most fitness businesses spend heavily to acquire new members while quietly losing existing ones. The real growth lever isn't more leads — it's stopping the bleed and converting what you already have.

Free Trials That Ghost

The average gym converts only 20–35% of free trials into paid memberships — not because the experience was bad, but because there was no structured follow-up after the trial ended. Trial members leave, life gets busy, and without a proactive outreach sequence, they quietly don't come back. Every unconverted trial is $600–$1,400 in annual membership revenue that walks out the door.

Members Going Inactive Before You Notice

The period between a member's last visit and the moment they cancel is typically 3–6 weeks — a window where intervention works. But most studios have no system to detect declining visit frequency until the cancellation request comes through. By then, the member has already mentally moved on. Catching the early warning signs and reaching out proactively is the difference between saving and losing that membership.

Manual Class Reminders and Check-Ins

No-shows to booked classes frustrate coaches, leave spots unfilled, and create scheduling chaos. Manually texting class reminders is time-consuming and inconsistent. Meanwhile, members who miss one week turn into members who miss a month — and then cancel. Automated, personalized touch-points keep members engaged between visits and dramatically reduce ghost bookings.

No Referral System

Referrals are the highest-quality, lowest-cost acquisition channel for any fitness studio — yet most gyms have no systematic way to generate them. A verbal ask at the front desk yields 1–3% referral rates. A structured, automated referral program with the right timing, the right incentive, and the right follow-up consistently generates 8–15% referral rates from your existing member base.

What We Automate

Six AI Systems That
Grow Your Membership

Every system is built around your membership tiers, your class schedule, and your studio's culture — not copy-pasted from a generic template.

Free Trial Nurture Sequence

From the moment a prospect starts their free trial, a personalized 10-day sequence engages them via SMS and email — welcoming them, highlighting class schedules, sharing success stories from current members, and presenting a compelling conversion offer on day 7. The sequence adapts based on how many classes they've attended. Studios using this system see 67% trial-to-member conversion, more than doubling the industry average.

Member Check-In & Engagement Automation

Weekly check-in messages celebrate member milestones — first month, 50th visit, 6-month anniversary — and keep your studio top of mind between sessions. Members who hit a streak get a congratulations message. Members who have upcoming renewals get a personalized value reminder. These small automated touches dramatically increase the emotional connection members feel with your studio and reduce passive cancellations.

At-Risk Member Intervention

Our system monitors visit frequency for every active member. When someone drops from 3+ visits per week to 1 or fewer — or misses their first two weeks in a row — an intervention sequence triggers automatically. The message is warm, not salesy: a genuine check-in from the studio, an offer to help them get back on track, and an easy re-engagement path. This system alone accounts for 41% of churn prevented.

Class & Appointment Reminders

Every booked class or personal training session triggers a reminder 24 hours and 2 hours in advance. Members confirm with a single reply, allowing your team to open waitlisted spots with enough lead time to fill them. No-shows to classes drop by 60–70% in the first 30 days. Coaches arrive prepared. Waitlisted members get off the bench. Your schedule runs as intended.

Referral Program Automation

We identify your highest-engagement members — those who visit frequently, have been members 90+ days, and have never cancelled — and invite them into your referral program at the right moment. They receive a unique referral link, an automated follow-up if they haven't used it, and a reward notification when their referral converts. Referral programs built on behavior-based timing outperform time-based campaigns by 4x.

Membership Renewal & Upsell Campaigns

Thirty days before a membership expires or auto-renews, members receive a personalized value summary — their total visits, classes attended, and milestones hit — paired with a renewal incentive or an upgrade offer to a higher-tier membership. Members who see their own progress are significantly more likely to renew or upgrade than those who receive a generic renewal notice.
