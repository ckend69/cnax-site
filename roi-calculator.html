About This Calculator

The calculator uses conservative, industry-validated assumptions. The 15% close rate on recovered leads is based on published research on lead follow-up response rates. Your actual numbers may vary — some businesses recover significantly more, some less. The goal is to give you a directional estimate, not an accounting report. The real exercise is making you think about costs you may have been ignoring.

The most common time-consuming tasks we automate include: lead response and qualification, appointment scheduling and reminders, quote or proposal follow-up, customer re-engagement and win-back campaigns, review request campaigns, data entry between systems, and routine customer communication like order status updates or payment reminders. The exact mix depends on your industry and business model.

Lead response automation typically shows results within the first week — you'll see more leads being engaged and more appointments being booked almost immediately. Full results from follow-up sequences and re-engagement campaigns typically materialize within 30–90 days. Most clients see a positive ROI within the first month.
