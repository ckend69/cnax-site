The Problem

The Leaks Costing Your Shop Real Money

Most auto repair shops lose thousands every month not from lack of customers — but from preventable friction no one has time to fix manually.

No-Shows and Last-Minute Cancellations

Every empty bay is money left on the floor. Without automated reminders and confirmations, no-show rates climb to 15–25% — and service advisors spend hours chasing down customers by phone.

Declined Repairs That Never Come Back

When a customer says "not today" on a brake job or timing belt, that ticket gets written off. Without a follow-up system, those deferred repairs walk out the door and into a competitor's bay.

Slow Review Generation

Happy customers rarely leave reviews on their own. Meanwhile one unhappy customer can post instantly. Without a systematic ask, your Google rating drifts lower than your actual service quality deserves.

No System for Service Interval Reminders

Customers who need oil changes, tire rotations, or brake inspections have no reason to call you — unless you call them first. Without mileage-based outreach, those intervals go missed and they drift to the quick-lube down the street.

What We Build

Six AI Systems That Run While You Work

Each system is purpose-built for auto repair workflows, integrated with your shop management software, and fully automated from day one.

Appointment Reminder Sequences

Multi-touch reminder sequences via SMS and email that confirm appointments 48 hours out, 24 hours out, and the morning of — with a one-click confirm or reschedule link. No-show rates drop an average of 71% within the first 60 days.

Declined Repair Follow-Up

When a customer declines a recommended repair, CNAX AI automatically triggers a follow-up sequence starting 7 days later. Messages reference the specific repair, include urgency cues for safety items, and offer easy one-click scheduling — recovering an average of $165K in deferred work annually.

Service Interval Reminders

Based on mileage captured at each visit, CNAX AI sends personalized reminders when customers are due for oil changes, tire rotations, brake inspections, and seasonal services. The right message reaches the right customer at exactly the right time.

Post-Service Review Requests

Within two hours of a completed repair, CNAX AI sends a personalized thank-you message with a direct link to your Google review page. Happy customers get asked at peak satisfaction — while the shop's service is still fresh in their mind. Shops see a 2.8x increase in review volume within 90 days.

Customer Reactivation Campaigns

Customers who haven't visited in 12 or more months receive a personalized win-back sequence with a specific service offer and a reminder of the vehicle they last brought in. Most shops recover 8–14% of dormant customers in the first campaign alone.

Seasonal Campaign Automation

Pre-built seasonal campaigns go out automatically each year — pre-winter safety checks, summer AC inspections, fall tire swap reminders, spring alignment specials. Set it once during onboarding and every campaign deploys on schedule without your team lifting a finger.
