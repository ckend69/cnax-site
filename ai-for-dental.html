The Real Problem

Your Schedule Has Holes.
Here's Why.

Most dental practices lose $80K–$250K per year not from a lack of patients — but from systems that let existing patients slip through the cracks.

The No-Show Epidemic

The average dental practice loses 18–23% of booked appointments to no-shows and last-minute cancellations. At $200–$400 per chair hour, that's tens of thousands in vanished revenue every single month — and a front desk scrambling to fill gaps manually.

Inactive Patient Lists Collecting Dust

The average practice has 400–900 patients who haven't been in over 18 months sitting dormant in their PMS. These aren't lost patients — they're patients who were never followed up with. Reactivating even 10% of them fills months of appointments without spending a dollar on ads.

Slow New Patient Follow-Up

When a prospective patient fills out a contact form or calls after hours, they're often comparing 3–5 practices simultaneously. Studies show 78% of patients book with the first provider that responds. If your team can't follow up within minutes, you're handing those patients to your competitors down the street.

Review Generation Is Manual and Forgotten

Your team finishes a great appointment, the patient is happy, and then nothing happens. Asking for reviews verbally is awkward, easy to forget, and wildly inconsistent. Meanwhile, practices with hundreds more Google reviews are capturing every new patient searching "dentist near me" — regardless of clinical quality.

What We Automate

Six AI Systems That
Fill Your Schedule

Every system is configured specifically for your practice, your PMS, and your patient communication style. No generic blasts.

Automated Appointment Reminders

Multi-step SMS and email reminder sequences deployed at 7 days, 48 hours, and 2 hours before each appointment. Patients confirm with a single reply. No-shows drop immediately, and your team stops making manual reminder calls that eat 60–90 minutes a day.

New Patient Lead Follow-Up

When a new patient inquiry comes in — from your website, Google, Facebook, or a referral partner — our AI responds within minutes, answers common questions, and guides them toward booking. You're alerted the moment they're ready to schedule, not after they've already called someone else.

Inactive Patient Reactivation

We identify every patient in your PMS who hasn't been seen in 18 months or more and run a warm, personalized reactivation sequence via SMS and email. The messaging is empathetic, not salesy — and it consistently converts 15–30% of contacted patients back into booked appointments within 30 days.

Review Request Automation

Triggered automatically 90 minutes after a completed appointment, a personalized review request goes out via SMS with a direct link to your Google Business Profile. Happy patients click and review in under 60 seconds. Unhappy patients are routed privately to your team. Average practices see 3x review velocity within 60 days.

Insurance & Benefits Reminders

Every October through December, we run targeted outreach to patients with unused insurance benefits expiring at year-end. Messages are personalized with their estimated remaining benefit amount, creating genuine urgency that drives Q4 appointment surges. This campaign alone recovers $40K–$80K in revenue for a typical practice.

Online Scheduling Confirmation

When a patient books online, an instant confirmation sequence fires across SMS and email with appointment details, directions, new patient forms, and a pre-appointment checklist. Patients arrive prepared, check-in faster, and the clinical team has everything they need before the patient walks through the door.
