The Problem

Where Law Firms Lose Clients Before They Start

Most law firms have a pipeline problem that looks like a marketing problem. The leads are there — the intake process is bleeding them out.

Slow Intake Killing Conversion

Research consistently shows that a prospect who submits a web inquiry is 10x more likely to retain a firm that responds within 5 minutes versus 30 minutes. Most firms respond in hours — if at all. By then, the prospect has already contacted two competitors.

Leads Going Cold Between Inquiry and Consult

A prospect fills out your contact form on a Monday, gets a call back on Wednesday, and schedules a consult for Friday. By Friday, their urgency has faded, they've gone with someone else, or they simply no-show. That gap costs law firms more retained clients than any other single factor.

Lawyers' Time Wasted on Unqualified Prospects

When intake is handled manually, attorneys and paralegals spend 30–45 minutes on consultations with prospects who were never a fit — wrong practice area, no case merit, outside jurisdiction. That time compounds across the week and represents some of the most expensive work in the firm.

No Systematic Follow-Up on Stale Leads

A prospect who inquired three months ago and went quiet isn't necessarily gone — their situation may have changed. Without a reactivation system, these leads sit dormant in a CRM while the firm pays for new leads to replace them. The cheapest client you'll ever retain is the one who already raised their hand.

What We Build

Six AI Systems That Fill Your Consultation Calendar

Purpose-built for legal intake workflows. Every system is configured for your practice areas, compliant with professional conduct standards, and fully automated from day one.

Instant Web Inquiry Response & Pre-Qualification

Within minutes of a form submission, CNAX AI responds with a personalized message and begins a structured pre-qualification sequence. It collects case-relevant information — incident date, jurisdiction, opposing party type, prior representation — so your staff reviews a qualified dossier, not a raw inquiry.

Consultation Scheduling Automation

Once pre-qualified, prospects receive a direct calendar link to book their consultation without going back and forth with your receptionist. Automated confirmations, 24-hour reminders, and a pre-consult preparation message reduce no-shows and ensure prospects arrive informed and ready to retain.

Stale Lead Reactivation

Leads that went cold after an inquiry or consult receive a re-engagement sequence at 30, 60, and 90-day intervals. Each message is context-aware — referencing the original inquiry type and offering a new entry point, such as a free case review or a relevant resource — to re-open the conversation without pressure.

Client Onboarding Document Collection

After a retainer is signed, CNAX AI automatically sends document collection reminders — intake forms, supporting records, prior correspondence — at set intervals until everything is received. Onboarding time shrinks from weeks to days, and your paralegals stop chasing clients for paperwork.

Referral Source Follow-Up

Referral partners — other attorneys, financial advisors, past clients — receive automated touchpoints that keep your firm top of mind without requiring your attorneys to make relationship maintenance calls. Systematic gratitude and status updates strengthen referral relationships and increase repeat referrals over time.

Review & Testimonial Collection

At case close, CNAX AI automatically sends a review request timed to peak client satisfaction. Messages are personalized by case type and outcome context, with direct links to Google, Avvo, or your preferred platform. Law firms see a 3x increase in positive reviews within the first quarter — directly impacting organic search visibility and referral credibility.
