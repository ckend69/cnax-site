The Real Problem

What's Draining Your Clinic

The average med spa loses 15–20% of its booked revenue each month to no-shows, slow re-booking, and missed upsell moments.

No-Shows Killing Revenue

A single no-show can cost $300–$800 in lost appointment revenue. Without automated reminders and confirmation workflows, no-shows are inevitable.

Clients Who Don't Come Back

Most med spa clients intend to return but never get re-engaged. Without a systematic re-booking process, you're constantly fighting for new clients instead of growing with existing ones.

Missed Upsell Opportunities

Your front desk is focused on check-in, not selling. Treatment package opportunities, add-ons, and product recommendations are left on the table at nearly every appointment.

Staff Overwhelmed by Admin

Confirming appointments, chasing deposits, answering FAQs, following up on inquiries — your team spends hours on tasks that AI can handle instantly and automatically.

What We Build

AI Automation Built for Aesthetic Clinics

Six intelligent systems that keep your schedule full, your clients loyal, and your revenue growing — without more staff.

AI Booking Assistant

Handle new appointment inquiries via text, Instagram DM, or web chat instantly — 24 hours a day. The AI qualifies clients, recommends treatments based on their goals, captures deposits, and locks in bookings without your front desk having to touch it.

No-Show Prevention

A smart multi-touch sequence confirms every appointment at 72 hours, 24 hours, and 2 hours before. If a client can't make it, the system automatically offers to reschedule — keeping the slot filled instead of empty. Shown to reduce no-shows by over 70%.

Client Re-engagement

Identify lapsed clients who haven't visited in 60, 90, or 180 days and automatically reach out with a personalized message and a compelling reason to return. Timed win-back campaigns with treatment-specific offers bring dormant clients back without cold calling.

Package Upsell Automation

After each appointment, AI sends a follow-up message at the optimal time — when client satisfaction is highest — recommending a complementary treatment or package upgrade. Personalized based on treatment history and stated goals. Revenue per client increases without pressure selling.

Review & Referral Sequences

Happy clients become your loudest advocates when prompted at the right moment. Our post-appointment AI sequence requests Google reviews from satisfied clients and plants a referral ask with a shareable offer — growing your reputation and new client pipeline on autopilot.

VIP Client Recognition

Automatically identify your highest-value clients by lifetime spend and visit frequency. Trigger exclusive early access to new treatments, birthday offers, and loyalty rewards — making your best clients feel like insiders and dramatically reducing churn at the top of your revenue pyramid.
