The Real Problem

Why Agents Lose Deals They Should Win

The average real estate lead is contacted by the first responding agent within 5 minutes — or not at all. Speed and persistence determine who closes, not who has the best listings.

Leads Going Cold in Minutes

Zillow, Realtor.com, and Facebook leads expect a response in under 5 minutes. When you're showing a home, in a meeting, or asleep, those leads contact another agent. The AI responds instantly — every time.

Hours Lost to Manual Follow-Up

Manually following up with 30–50 leads at different stages of the buying journey is a full-time job. Without automation, most agents give up after 2 touches — missing the 80% of deals that close after touch 5 or later.

Long-Cycle Leads Falling Through Cracks

Real estate buyers often take 6–18 months to transact. Without a structured nurture sequence, those leads go silent — and end up closing with an agent who stayed in touch.

Referrals Not Being Systematically Requested

Most agents get referrals by luck. A post-closing referral sequence and a past-client re-engagement campaign would generate predictable referral volume — but building and running one manually is nearly impossible.

What We Build

AI Systems That Work Your Pipeline

Six automation systems that respond instantly, qualify intelligently, nurture persistently, and generate referrals systematically — all without adding to your workload.

Instant Lead Response

Every lead from Zillow, Realtor.com, your website, Facebook, or any other source gets an immediate, personalized AI response in under 90 seconds — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The message references the specific property they inquired on, asks qualifying questions, and keeps the conversation going while you're unavailable. First-mover advantage on every single lead.

Buyer & Seller Qualification

The AI asks the right questions — pre-approval status, timeline, price range, motivation, current living situation — and scores each lead by readiness and fit. Hot leads get escalated to you immediately with a full profile. Lookers and long-cycle leads enter a nurture sequence so nothing is wasted. You only spend time with people ready to move.

Showing Automation

Once a lead is qualified, the AI handles the scheduling conversation — offering available showing times, confirming the appointment, and sending reminders at 24 hours and 2 hours before. Confirmation rates increase dramatically and no-shows drop. Your calendar fills with confirmed, qualified showings — not tentative maybe's that evaporate.

Long-Term Nurture Sequences

6–18 month nurture sequences for buyers not ready to act yet. Market updates, neighborhood spotlights, interest rate commentary, and personalized check-ins delivered at intelligent intervals — keeping you top of mind until the moment they're ready to buy or sell. Most of your competitors give up after 2 follow-ups. You don't have to.

Listing Alert Automation

When a new listing hits the market that matches a buyer's criteria, the AI sends an immediate, personalized alert — with property details, a link, and a scheduling prompt. Buyers feel personally served. You're always the agent who found them the right home first. Works for active buyer clients and prospects in your long-term nurture pool.

Referral & Repeat Client Outreach

After every closing, a post-transaction sequence thanks the client, collects a testimonial, and plants a referral ask at the optimal moment — when satisfaction is highest. Annual check-ins go out every year on the home anniversary with a market update and a subtle invitation to refer. Your past clients become a predictable referral engine.
