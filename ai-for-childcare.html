The Problem

What's Keeping Your Spots Empty and Your Staff Overwhelmed

Childcare centers lose families not because of program quality — but because the enrollment process is slow, manual, and inconsistent. Meanwhile, your staff's time is being swallowed by communication tasks instead of care.

Slow Inquiry Response Losing Families to Competitors

Parents researching childcare are typically contacting three to five centers simultaneously. The first center to respond with warmth and a clear next step wins the tour. When your inquiry response takes 24–48 hours, families have already toured and enrolled elsewhere — regardless of how good your program is.

Waitlist Families Going Cold

A family that joined your waitlist six months ago was interested then. Without regular touchpoints, they've enrolled elsewhere, forgotten about you, or lost confidence that a spot is actually coming. Most centers have no system for nurturing waitlist families, so when a spot opens, the family is gone and the spot sits empty longer than it should.

Tour No-Shows

A family that schedules a tour is a high-intent prospect — but without reminders, 20–35% of scheduled tours result in no-shows. Each missed tour is a lost enrollment opportunity and wasted time for your director. Manual reminder calls aren't scalable when your admin team is already stretched thin.

Parent Communication Bottlenecks

Enrolled parents expect timely, consistent communication — program updates, re-enrollment reminders, policy changes, event notices. When these are handled manually, they're inconsistent and delayed. Your directors end up spending hours every week on communication tasks that could run on autopilot, taking time away from what they were hired to do.

What We Build

Six AI Systems That Fill Spots and Keep Families Engaged

Every system is built for the specific rhythms of childcare — enrollment seasons, waitlist dynamics, parent communication expectations, and re-enrollment cycles.

Instant Enrollment Inquiry Response

Within minutes of a parent submitting an inquiry — through your website, Facebook, or Google — CNAX AI responds with a warm, personalized message that introduces your center, answers the most common initial questions, and invites the family to schedule a tour. Response time drops from hours to under 5 minutes, and inquiry-to-tour conversion jumps immediately.

Tour Scheduling & Reminder Automation

From the initial response, parents receive a direct link to book a tour on your calendar — no back-and-forth emails or phone tag. Once scheduled, CNAX AI sends a confirmation, a 48-hour reminder, and a same-day reminder with parking instructions and what to expect. Tour no-show rates drop by over 60%, and directors reclaim hours previously spent on tour coordination logistics.

Waitlist Nurture Sequences

Families on your waitlist receive a carefully spaced sequence of touchpoints — center updates, staff spotlights, program highlights, and milestone check-ins — that keep your center top of mind throughout the wait. When a spot opens, the family is warm, engaged, and ready to enroll the same week. Centers that run waitlist nurture see spot-to-enrollment time shrink from 3–4 weeks to under 5 days.

Enrolled Family Communication & Re-Enrollment

Enrolled parents receive consistent, timely communication through automated sequences — monthly program updates, upcoming event reminders, curriculum highlights, and seasonal announcements. Re-enrollment campaigns deploy automatically 90 days before the new enrollment year with early-bird reminders and a simple one-click re-enrollment link, dramatically reducing the last-minute scramble every spring.

Review Collection from Happy Parents

Three to four weeks after enrollment — when new families have settled in and experienced the program — CNAX AI sends a review request with a direct Google link and a warm message that makes it easy to share feedback. Centers using this system see a 3–5x increase in review volume within 60 days, improving local search rankings and organic inquiry volume over time.

Referral Program for Current Families

Your enrolled families are your most credible source of new enrollments. CNAX AI runs an automated referral program that invites parents to share your center with friends and family, makes referring simple with a personalized link, and tracks referrals automatically. Centers with active referral programs fill 25–35% of new spots through family referrals — the lowest-cost, highest-trust enrollment channel available.
